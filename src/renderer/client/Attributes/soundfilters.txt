/************************************************************************
**
** SOUNDFILTER
**
** The Sound Filter definitions define all of the attributes associated 
** with a particular sound filter type.  Each sound filter, by definition,
** contains 0 to 10 Variable, Value pairs.  For each variable definition
** there MUST be a corresponding value.  For example,
**
**		Var0	= "Quality"
**		Value0	= 1.0
**		Var1	= "Level"
**		Value1	= 3.0
**
**	is valid.  However,
**
**		Var0	= "Quality"
**		Var1	= "Level"
**		Value1	= 3.0"
**
**	is NOT valid.	
**
** All SoundFilter definitions must contain the following properties:
**
**  Name (String)
**
**      The user friendly name of the sound filter (what shows up in DEdit). 
**
**  FilterName (String)
**
**      The name of the filter. 
**
**  Var0 - Var9 (String)
**
**      Variable names associated with the filter. 
**
**	Value0 - Value9 (Float)
**
**		The values of the above Var0 - Var9 variables.
**
*/

/*
**	Parameters
**
**	These are the parameters that are available for each filter type with
**	ranges and default values.
**
**	Values are given with units for reference, where appropriate, but only enter the 
**	floating point version of the number when you actually set the value.
**
**	
**	Reverb:
**	"Environment"		0			25		One of the EAX2.0 Environment values.
**	"Room"				-10000mb	0mb		Attenuation of room effect
**	"RoomHF"			-10000mb	0mb		Attenuation of high frequency room effect
**	"RoomRolloffFactor"	0			10		Rolloff for reflected signals
**	"DecayTime"			0.1ms		20ms	Decay time (Duh!)		
**	"DecayHFRatio"		0.1			2.0		Ratio of decay of high-frequency to low-frequency
**	"Reflections"		-10000mb	1000mb	Attenuation of early reflections relative to room effect
**	"ReflectionsDelay"	0ms			0.3ms	Delay of early reflection relative to direct sound
**	"Reverb"			-10000mb	2000mb	Attenuation of late reflections relative to room effect
**	"ReverbDelay"		0ms			0.1ms	Delay of late reflections
**	"Diffusion"			0			1		Echo Size in later reflection decay
**	"Size"				1m			100m	Size of room
**	"AirAbsorptionHF" 	-100		0		Air absorption of HF
**	"Direct"			-10000		1000	Direct sound (dry sound) boost (reduce for greater reverb effect)
**
*/

*/
